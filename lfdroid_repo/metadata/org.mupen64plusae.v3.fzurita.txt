Description:
Mupen64Plus AE (Android Edition) is a port of Mupen64Plus to Android (not officially supported by the [http://mupen64plus.org/ Mupen64Plus] team). See [http://pdroms.de/files/nintendo64 PDRoms] for public domain ROMs.

For info about specific games: [http://mupen64plus.wikia.com/wiki/Mupen64Plus,_Android_Edition_%28AE%29_Wiki compatibility wiki].

Getting started:
* Transfer ROMs anywhere to your internal storage (default /sdcard/roms/n64/)
* Browse to them from within the app
* ROMs must be in .n64, .v64, or .z64 format, optionally in .zip files
.
